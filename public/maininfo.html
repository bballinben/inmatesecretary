Connecting to the outside world.

Learn how we connect you and your loved ones.

We understand new challenges arise when a family member, loved one, or friend is incarcerated. Here at Inmate Secretary we offer many services to provide solutions for those challenges. Our unique business model helps us leverage current systems that inmates utilize so we can provide services that make life easier.

These services include Social Media Management, Pen Pal Services, Release and Transition Services, Product Sales, Photo Services, and Email Management Services. Inmate Secretary is not limited to these services. We will fulfill any request that is reasonable and law-abiding.


*Disclaimer* Inmate Secretary will not assist in any illegal activities, contact individuals on a no contact list, and will always abide by local, state and federal laws and policies.


How do we offer these services despite our clients being incarcerated?

The process to become one of our clients is easy and hassle free. First, contact us and request a packet. It is important to fill out all information honestly. Next, choose a pricing package. Which package you choose determines what services will be provided. Lastly, we will fulfill the services you’ve purchased and keep you updated via email.

We would love to answer any questions you have about our services. Visit our contact section to leave us a message.
